Lawn Sprinkler Controller

One of my favorite projects to date.

Goal:
Design a system that could interface with the sprinkler zone system in the house, save a schedule, and turn sprinkler zones on/off at the proper times. The UI should be friendly and easy to understand, and the device should be reliable even when left alone for long periods of time.

Materials used:
Arduino, Arduino IDE, LCD display, relays, keypad, timing module, and more.

Challenges:
I ran into a couple challenges along the way. One example was creating a functional and usable UI given the limitations of the display and keypad. Another was properly keeping time, which lead to me adding the timing module.

Result:
In the end, the project ended up being quite successful. Testing the system went well and it was able to properly store a schedule, then run it over the course of a week. The main driving factor behind the project was to replace an old system that was horrible, and I can confidently say the new one was better.
